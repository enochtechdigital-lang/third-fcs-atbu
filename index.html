About Us

We are a campus fellowship committed to raising godly leaders, nurturing spiritual growth and transforming lives through discipleship, leadership development and kingdom impact within our university community.

Our Vision

To be a well-developed and equipped ministry with adequate resources for holistic ministry among young people and families, promoting a godly society.

Our Mission

To evangelize, disciple, and provide leadership training, livelihood, and psychosocial support to pupils, students, other youths, and families.

Sermons & Messages

The Prepared,Equipped Witness.
We are not on campus by accident. We are not here just to earn degrees, write exams, and graduate. We are here on assignment. Before a witness can speak boldly, he must be prepared. Before a witness can stand firmly, she must be equipped. Preparation happens in the place of prayer. Equipping happens through the Word. Strength comes from fellowship. Boldness comes from the Spirit. We are being shaped, refined, and trained — not just for meetings, but for moments. Moments in lecture halls. Moments in hostels. Moments in conversations that could change someone’s eternity. To be a witness is not just to talk about Christ — it is to live Him, reflect Him, and represent Him. We are prepared in character. We are equipped in truth. We are empowered for impact. Campus is not just a place of academics. It is a mission field. And we are ready. Prepared. Equipped. Witness.
